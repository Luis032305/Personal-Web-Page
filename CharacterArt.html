Wall-nut (Plants vs Zombies)

Wall-nut is calm by nature and slow to panic. He knows his shell is strong and he trusts it. He stands in front to shield the plants behind him, taking hits so others stay safe.
Health: 400
Role: Defensive barrier
Sun Cost: 50
Special: Blocks zombies and absorbs damage
